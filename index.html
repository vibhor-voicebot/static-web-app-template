Snaptrics Lifesciences

Snaptrics Lifesciences is a leading medical warehouse of all latest medical products, tools, equipment, supplies, medicines & healthcare benefits. Snaptrics Medical Warehouse (SMW) is a real time database that consolidates data from a variety of medical sources, outlets, stores & clinics to present a unified view of a available medical resources. A SMW could be used in the hospital setting to track prescribing trends as well as for the monitoring of infectious diseases.
